Localização

Vitoria-Gasteiz, the capital of the Basque Country and Álava, is located in northern Spain at 525 meters above sea level. Surrounded by mountains and green areas like the Green Belt, it has an oceanic-continental climate. It is an important administrative, cultural, and transportation hub with a well-preserved medieval old town.
