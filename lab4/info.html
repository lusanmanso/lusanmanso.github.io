Facts about Vitoria-Gasteiz

  • It's the capital of the Basque Country and Álava province.
  • The Green Belt is a natural area surrounding the city, perfect for walks.
  • The city has a rich history reflected in its medieval old town.
  • Vitoria-Gasteiz was named European Green Capital in 2012.
  • Virgen Blanca Square is a central and iconic spot in the city.
